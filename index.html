THE MENU

Slow-cooked proteins piled high into fresh, warm rolls. Built for speed, born from flavor.

12-hour chuck roast slow-braised in a rich blend of guajillo and ancho chiles. Stuffed into a toasted bolillo roll with melted mozzarella, sweet white onions, and fresh cilantro. Served with a side of piping hot consommé for dipping. Messy, rich, and worth every napkin.
📷 [Image: Chicken Tinga Sub]
Chicken Tinga$13.50
Tender shredded chicken thighs simmered low and slow in a smoky chipotle-adobo sauce with crushed tomatoes and garlic. Loaded with cool avocado crema, pickled jalapeños for a bright kick, and crumbled cotija cheese.

Italian Beef$14.50
Our spin on the classic. Juicy chuck roast slow-cooked in a house Italian spice blend and sharp pepperoncini brine. Piled high on bread, completely dipped in hot savory jus, and loaded with spicy giardiniera and roasted sweet peppers.
📷 [Image: Gochujang Pulled Pork]
Gochujang Pulled Pork$13.50
Sweet meets heat. Tender pork shoulder glazed in a sticky, savory reduction of gochujang, soy, ginger, and brown sugar. Balanced beautifully with a crunchy sesame slaw, cool cucumber slices, and fresh scallions.
📷 [Image: Bigos Braised Pork]
Bigos Braised Pork$13.50
A heavy-hitting nod to traditional Polish comfort food. Pork shoulder slow-simmered with old-school sauerkraut, smoky kielbasa chunks, juniper berries, and marjoram. Finished with a swipe of grainy mustard and a sharp dill pickle wedge.
📷 [Image: Lentil & Mushroom Sub]
Gochujang Lentil (V)$12.50
Who says a great braise needs meat? Plump, earthy lentils and sliced cremini mushrooms simmered down in our signature sweet-and-spicy gochujang reduction. Topped with our crunchy sesame slaw, cucumbers, and scallions.
