FOND is Cooking.

We simmer premium proteins low and slow until they are incredibly tender, then pile them high into fresh, warm rolls. No shortcuts. No compromise. Built on patience, born from flavor.

Our Progress Pipeline

  • Phase 1: Concept & Recipe Dev (Complete)

    Perfecting our signature slow-braises, selecting regional bakers, and curating our launch menu lineup.

  • Phase 2: Market Testing (In Progress)

    Proving the concept on the ground. We are opening temporary farmer's market stalls and hosting limited pop-up events to get our food out to the community before we even have wheels.

  • Phase 3: Building the Beast

    Using the funding generated from our Kickstarter campaign and structural market testing to purchase a physical truck and outfit it with high-end, professional kitchen gear.

  • Phase 4: Fully Operational

    Spreading FOND to the community. Transitioning to a permanent weekly schedule, catering large events, and feeding hungry neighborhoods across the region.
